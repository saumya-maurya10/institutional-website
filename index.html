College Life

College Life at Riverdale

Hostel Life

Life in the Riverdale hostels is vibrant, safe, and full of opportunities for personal growth. Students from diverse backgrounds live together, creating a dynamic and inclusive environment.

  • Separate hostels for boys and girls with 24/7 security
  • Comfortable rooms with Wi-Fi and study areas
  • Hygienic mess providing nutritious meals
  • Common rooms with TV, indoor games, and recreational activities
  • Regular cultural and sports activities inside hostels

Hostel life helps students build friendships, learn independence, and develop essential life skills.

Cultural Fest - Riviera

Riviera is the grand annual cultural festival of Riverdale Institute of Technology. It is one of the most awaited events of the year, attracting participants from colleges across the country.

  • Music concerts, dance battles, and drama performances
  • Fashion shows, art exhibitions, and literary competitions
  • Technical events, coding contests, and hackathons
  • Celebrity appearances and live shows
  • Food stalls, fun games, and campus-wide celebrations

Riviera showcases talent, creativity, and the energetic spirit of students, making campus life exciting and memorable.

Entrance Exam

Entrance Exam Information

Exam Name

Riverdale Engineering Entrance Test (REET)

Eligibility

  • Must have completed 12th with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
  • Minimum 50% marks

Exam Pattern

  • Mode: Online
  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
  • Type: Multiple Choice Questions

Important Dates

  • Application Start: May 1
  • Exam Date: June 15
  • Results: June 30
